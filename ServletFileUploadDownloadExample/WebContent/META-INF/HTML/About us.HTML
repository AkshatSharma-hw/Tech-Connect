What we do?

Health wallet is an idea to minimize the use of papers in the Medical Industry. This idea allows the user to keep their health related documents on the cloud and thus helps them to use it in the case of emergency. Health wallet also helps to manage the documents in various different ways and also gives them a reminder and disease-related data from the net. It will also be comfortable for the doctors to have a complete case history of the patient he is working with and if it is the case if emergency then the can also look up to their medical history so that they can actually select the best medication suitable for the particular patient. Even if the patient is unconscious doctors can use their identification proves to see which kind of treatment they are going through. This idea will especially help the patients with rare diseases.

Keywords- Rare diseases: A disease or disorder condition with a prevalence of 1 or less, per 1000 population is defined by the World Health Organization (WHO) as a rare disease. (“Around 80 million people in India suffer from "life threatening" rare diseases”-NDTV)
